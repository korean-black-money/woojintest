Calibrate

About Calibrate

Calibrate is a lightweight nutrition and fitness planning site built to give users a clearer starting point for calorie targets, macro balance, training direction, and nearby fitness venue discovery.

What The Site Does

The calculator combines body data, goal selection, training frequency, and daily activity level to generate a simple plan. It then connects that plan to nearby fitness venues within a three-mile radius so users can move from “what should I do?” to “where can I actually do it?” in one session.

What The Site Does Not Do

Calibrate does not diagnose, treat, or manage medical conditions. It is not a replacement for medical care, registered dietitian advice, or individualized coaching.

Our Intent

The site is designed to be useful before it tries to monetize. The main goal is to publish a tool people can return to because it is practical, easy to use, and more actionable than a generic calculator.
