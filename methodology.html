Calibrate

Methodology

Calibrate uses a practical wellness-planning approach rather than a medical or clinical model. The tool estimates calorie needs, distributes macros into a simple starting split, and builds a training suggestion around the user’s stated goal and activity profile.

Inputs Used

  • Age, biological sex, height, and weight
  • Daily activity level
  • Training frequency
  • Goal selection such as fat loss, maintenance, muscle gain, or performance
  • Location permission or a searched area for venue discovery

Venue Ranking

Nearby venues are filtered to a three-mile radius. Ranking is then influenced by the user’s goal, likely workout profile, venue type, distance, and available quality signals. The same ranked order is used on-page and in optional email delivery.

Limitations

Results are estimates, not prescriptions. Body composition, medical history, medication use, injury status, and recovery capacity all affect real-world needs.
