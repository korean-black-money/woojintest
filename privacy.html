Calibrate

Privacy Policy

Calibrate collects the values a user enters into the calculator for the purpose of generating a nutrition and training result on the page. Optional email delivery is only used when a user chooses to submit an email address for plan delivery.

Information Used

  • Age, sex, height, weight, activity level, training frequency, and goal selection
  • Approximate location data only when the user grants browser permission or performs a manual search
  • An optional email address when the user asks to receive their plan by email

Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on site usage and general advertising preferences.

Third-Party Services

The site uses Google Maps and Places data for location-based venue discovery and EmailJS for optional email delivery.

User Choice

Users can avoid optional email delivery by leaving the email field blank, and they can avoid location sharing by searching a place manually instead of granting browser location access.
